Suruli Greens Product Catalog

Welcome to the Suruli Greens product management system. This page provides access to the Google Sheet where you can manage your product catalog.

How to Use the Product Catalog

The Google Sheet allows you to:

  • Update product categories
  • Add new items to each category
  • Edit product names, descriptions, and prices
  • Update product availability
Note: Changes made to the Google Sheet will be reflected on your website automatically. Make sure to follow the structure of the sheet to ensure proper display on the website.
